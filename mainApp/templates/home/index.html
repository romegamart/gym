About Us SAM STRENGTH FITNESS – Unisex Gym

Sameer Kumar – Master Trainer, Personal Trainer, Fitness Trainer & Certified Nutritionist (Food & Supplementation). Whether you need Weight Gain, Weight Loss, Muscle Building, Competitive Bodybuilding, or Men’s Physique Preparation, we deliver science-backed results.
