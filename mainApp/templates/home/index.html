About Us SAM STRENGTH FITNESS – Unisex Gym

Sameer Rajput – Master Trainer, Personal Trainer, Fitness Trainer & Certified Nutritionist (Food & Supplementation). With 16 years of experience, he is a decorated champion: Mr India, Mr North India, Mr Delhi, Mr Haryana. He has trained athletes in Bodybuilding, Fitness Modelling, and Men’s Physique.

Our gym SAM STRENGTH FITNESS UNISEX GYM located at FS-5, Second Floor, above Axis Bank, Tagore Garden, Delhi-110027, is built for real transformations. Whether you need Weight Gain, Weight Loss, Muscle Building, Competitive Bodybuilding, or Men’s Physique Preparation, we deliver science-backed results.
